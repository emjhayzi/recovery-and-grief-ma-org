# Release Roadmap

## Recommended Delivery Cadence

For this project, a 1-week sprint cadence is the best fit. The team is small, the codebase is lightweight, and most remaining work depends on decisions and approvals rather than heavy engineering.

## Phase 0: Current State

- Status: complete
- Outcome: first structured website draft exists
- Notes: the foundation is strong enough to move into controlled launch prep

## Sprint 1: Content Lock And Support Pathways

Primary goal:
Turn the most important placeholder areas into real, usable support pathways.

Target outcomes:

- direct contact workflow finalized
- one-on-one pathway clarified
- group joining instructions finalized
- homepage and footer CTAs aligned

## Sprint 2: Trust And Content Completion

Primary goal:
Raise confidence, credibility, and emotional safety across the site.

Target outcomes:

- team bios and partner details approved
- curated resources added
- whole-person page clarified
- volunteer-page decision finalized
- placeholder language reduced or removed

## Sprint 3: Launch Readiness

Primary goal:
Prepare the project for public release.

Target outcomes:

- mobile and desktop QA complete
- accessibility review complete on final content
- publish checklist completed
- hosting and DNS plan approved
- deployment workflow tested

## Sprint 4: Go Live And Stabilize

Primary goal:
Launch safely and capture follow-up work.

Target outcomes:

- website deployed to production
- live domain tested
- any form/contact routing verified
- initial backlog of post-launch improvements captured

## Post-Launch Iteration

Focus areas:

- analytics and search-console setup if approved
- SEO refinements
- content updates from real program activity
- volunteer pathway expansion if needed
- maintenance-retainer or handoff model implementation

## Milestone Summary

- Milestone 1: Content-critical launch blockers resolved
- Milestone 2: Public-facing trust and resource content approved
- Milestone 3: Technical publish path approved and tested
- Milestone 4: Live launch complete
