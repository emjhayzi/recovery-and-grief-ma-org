You Are Not Alone

Everyone's journey with alcohol and other drugs is different.

Regardless of where you're at, where you've been, or how you got here, if you are grieving the substance-use-related death of someone you cared about deeply, you are welcome here. Our common bond is our grief over the loss of a friend, family member, or anyone who was part of our lives. You are not alone in your sorrow and suffering.

We invite you to choose a starting place.
