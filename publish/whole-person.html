Your Path

Find Your Recovery Path

This page shares information about the New Form approach to helping every individual in recovery see themselves as the whole person they truly are and to heal in their own way. We are more than our addiction, and the lives of those who have died were about more than what ended their lives. For more information: path@recoveryandgrief-MA.org

New Form and other paths to recovery

New Form is built on the belief that connection counters addiction. Founded by Scott Strode — who started The Phoenix more than 20 years ago — New Form is described as a "community of communities" with the unifying goal of optimal mental wellness and recovery. And as Scott says, "the beauty of New Form is it's free."

Learn about New Form and other paths to recovery that can support you as you grieve and heal.
