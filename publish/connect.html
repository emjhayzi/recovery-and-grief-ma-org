Connect With a Peer Grief Helper

If you think you might benefit from talking to someone who has "been there," please email us at connect@recoveryandgrief-MA.org and we will reach out to you.

Who we are

We are not clinicians or professional counselors. We are Peer Grief Helpers who provide compassionate support to grieving people to help them find their way. Every staff person and volunteer who does outreach has lived through their own story of losing someone they deeply care about to alcohol or other drugs, and we are all trained in the Peer Support Community Partners RIVER Peer Grief Support Model.

By connecting with a Peer Grief Helper, you will be able to tell your story to someone who knows what the pain of this kind of grief is like — and who will listen without opinions, pressure, or judgment. Peer Grief Helpers have traveled their own grief journey and found hope and healing. They meet you where you are. Each of them will help you find your personal path to healing.

A single email is enough to begin. You do not need to explain everything at once.

Get Help Soon

Through our outreach programs — Grief Connections and Finding Your Way — grieving people are contacted by a Peer Grief Helper who listens with compassion and gives information and support according to each person's particular needs.

  • Grief Connections works with local agencies to reach out and provide timely, personalized assistance to grievers
  • Finding Your Way responds to individuals who contact SADOD directly
  • You can start with one question or one short message

Get Ongoing Help

Through the Peer Grief Ally Program, a bereaved person and their Ally work together on the issues most important to the griever. Allies are volunteers trained to support grieving people by having conversations about what each person most needs and wants to talk about.

  • Ongoing, one-on-one peer grief support over time
  • The tragedies shared by Ally and griever create a foundation of common ground
  • A mutually healing experience grounded in lived experience
