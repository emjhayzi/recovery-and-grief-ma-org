Crisis Support

Get Help Now

If you need immediate support, start here

If you or someone else is in immediate danger, call 911 now or go to the nearest emergency room. If you are in emotional distress or suicidal crisis in the United States, call or text 988 to reach the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline.

Call or Text 988 Visit 988 Online

The 988 Lifeline is available 24/7. If you are outside the United States, use your local emergency number or crisis service.

Immediate Danger

If there is a life-threatening emergency, do not wait for a callback or email response. Call 911 right away or go to the nearest emergency room.
